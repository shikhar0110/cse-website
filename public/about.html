About IIIT Guwahati

The Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati (IIITG) is an institute of national importance established in 2013. It was set up by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, and the Government of Assam as a public-private partnership with the goal of producing high-quality technical professionals in information technology and related fields.

Vision

IIIT Guwahati aims to be an institution that fosters technical excellence, creativity, and innovation in information technology, serving as a center of knowledge and expertise in the Northeast and the country as a whole.

Mission

Our mission is to impart top-tier education in information technology and allied fields, conduct advanced research, and address the challenges faced by society through collaborative projects and outreach.

Programs Offered

IIIT Guwahati offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in disciplines such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and related fields. Each program is designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and hands-on skills.

Facilities

  • Modern Campus: Our state-of-the-art campus is equipped with smart classrooms, high-speed internet, and advanced laboratories.
  • Library: The IIITG library has a rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support learning and research.
  • Hostels: Comfortable accommodation facilities are available for students within the campus.
  • Sports and Recreation: Facilities for sports and other recreational activities contribute to a balanced lifestyle for students.

Research and Innovation

IIIT Guwahati is committed to advancing knowledge and innovation in information technology and allied fields. Our research centers focus on cutting-edge areas like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and more. Faculty and students work on projects funded by national and international agencies, contributing significantly to advancements in technology.
